Who We're Looking For

The signals we look for

We're not looking for the loudest voice. We're looking for the most credible one.

✓
MSP operator with hands-on experience
Currently works in IT or MSP as an owner, engineer, technician, or consultant — with real experience running or supporting managed services.
✓
Actively experimenting with AI
Not just curious about AI — testing it, building with it, or applying it in your own MSP operations. That is the most important signal we look for.
✓
Active voice in the MSP community
A consistent history contributing to MSP communities — LinkedIn, forums, user groups, events. You contribute, not just consume.
✓
Independent thinker
You're publicly associated with industry-wide MSP ideas. You ask questions, challenge norms, and the industry actually listens to you.
✓
Community-first mindset
Genuine passion for peer-led learning and helping other MSPs grow. You know the difference between building a community and running a marketing channel.
✓
Able to commit 4–6 hours per month
Active, not performative. Contributing consistently, anchored to real impact, running in an MSP or actively influencing MSP culture.
Process

Six steps from application to impact

Applications take less than 5 minutes. Rolling review — we'll be in touch within 5 business days.

Step 01
Apply & Get Selected
Submit your application highlighting how you contribute to the MSP ecosystem and your preferred track. No time wasted — we'll be in touch within a few days.
Step 02
Onboard & Get Ready
Join your track, meet the team, and get your ambassador toolkit. Short onboarding, zero fluff. Enough context to start making an impact from day one.
Step 03
Engage
Pick your track and actively participate in forums, meetups, webinars, and advocacy. Contribute at your own pace.
Step 04
Activate
The best ambassadors already have a built-in advocacy impulse. We remove the friction — and help you connect with peers, run events, and build momentum.
Step 05
Recognize & Reward
Contributions celebrated through spotlights, credentials, perks, and early product access. Ambassador feedback continuously shapes the SuperOps roadmap.
Step 06
Evolve Together
The program grows. Tracks and coverage expand. Ranked cohorts go first as new benefits, territories, and opportunities — like the AI Marketplace — come to life.
