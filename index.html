Process

How the Program Works

A clear, six-step journey from application to ongoing impact — designed to respect your time and maximize the value you get from the program.

→
Step 01

Apply

Submit a short application outlining your role, experience, and preferred ambassador track. No long essays — just a clear picture of who you are and how you contribute.

→
Step 02

Review & Select

The SuperOps team reviews applications on a rolling basis using clear eligibility criteria and product-usage data to choose a small, high-fit founding cohort.

→
Step 03

Onboard

Complete a short survey, then join a 1:1 onboarding call to align on goals and ideal activities. Receive a welcome pack and full program overview.

→
Step 04

Engage

Pick your track — community, technical, or content. Each quarter, participate in a mix of events, feedback sessions, and advocacy activities at your own pace.

→
Step 05

Recognize & Reward

Contributions are celebrated through spotlights, credentials, perks, and early product access — while your feedback continuously shapes the SuperOps roadmap.

Step 06

Evolve Together

Program performance is reviewed quarterly. Both tracks and benefits are refined based on what delivers the most value — to ambassadors and to SuperOps.

Community Ambassador Framework

The SUPER Model

A companion framework that keeps the day-to-day program simple, focused, and built around the people who matter most — your ambassadors.

S
Source
Source the Right Ambassadors
Identify high-fit customers using product usage, NPS scores, CSM nominations, and community activity. Focus on MSP owners, engineers, and architects who already champion SuperOps.
U
Understand
Understand Their Goals
Use a short survey and a 1:1 onboarding call to uncover each ambassador's goals, constraints, and preferred ways to contribute before asking anything of them.
P
Pilot
Pilot with a Small Cohort
Start with a curated founding group to test tracks, calibrate expectations, and validate the program's value proposition before scaling.
E
Engage
Engage with Meaningful Tracks
Offer clear opt-in tracks — community, technical, content, reviews, and product feedback — with a light, predictable quarterly cadence that respects busy schedules.
R
Recognize
Recognize and Refine
Publicly celebrate contributions, offer credentials and perks, and continuously refine the experience based on ambassador feedback and measurable business impact.
